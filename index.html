About Us

We Are Pt. Export Tani Nusantara

PT Expor Tani Nusantara is a company dedicated to exporting high-quality Indonesian products, including spices, agricultural produce, coffee, and healthy chips, to international markets. This is in line with our mission to "Bridge UMKM to the Worldwide Market." Our commitment is to:

  1. Empower local farmers to achieve prosperity through sustainable farming practices, fair pricing, and enhanced welfare.
  2. Enable esteemed B2B customers to meet their business objectives by supplying superior Indonesian products.
  3. Offer our valued end customers the benefits of our products, including unique flavors, enhanced well-being, and a healthier lifestyle.
  4. Foster a positive and inclusive work environment where our dedicated team can thrive, grow, and develop professionally. thrives in a positive and inclusive work environment that encourages growth and development.

Global demand for ethically sourced agricultural products is increasing, according to the FAO. In response, we partner with local Indonesian farmers to provide premium Wamena coffee, authentic spices, agriculture, and healthy chips. Founded in 2022, we combine deep agricultural knowledge with modern processing to become a trusted exporter of quality Indonesian products worldwide.
